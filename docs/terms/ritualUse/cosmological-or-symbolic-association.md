## Definition

Represents symbolic, cosmological, or metaphysical associations without direct procedural ritual usage.

---

## Scope note

Used when a plant carries meaning within mythic, cosmological, or symbolic frameworks but is not necessarily tied to a specific enacted rite in this dataset.
